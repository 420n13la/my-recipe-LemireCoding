Here is how to make my favorite reciepe:

My favorite reciepe is one I created at home.
It consists of using a peta bread to make a pizza covered of feta cheese.

List of ingredients you will need ( I created the reciepe so I never really took note of the quantity required):

  • Peta bread
  • Feta cheese
  • The sauce of your choice
  • Proschutto
  • Tomato Salad

Fallow this easy reciepe to make your own Feta cheese!

  1. Add your desired sauce on the Peta bread
  2. Add the Tomato Salad
  3. Add Proschutto on top as If it is peperonni
  4. Add as much feta cheese as necessary
  5. Put in the oven for 10 minutes at 350*
